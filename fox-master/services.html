Services

What We Offer

We provide comprehensive solutions tailored to your property needs, ensuring hassle-free management and expert guidance. From property maintenance to intellectual property services, we deliver excellence every step of the way. Trust us to simplify your property journey with professionalism and care

Property Investigation Services

At Skylight, we provide thorough property investigation services to help clients make informed decisions. Our experts physically inspect properties to uncover issues such as pathway problems, legal disputes, or encumbrances. We assess market value, future appreciation, and any ongoing or potential land acquisition proceedings. Additionally, we verify surrounding developments that may impact the property's value. Our team includes Engineers,retired judges,Civil advocates, Tahsildars , VAOs, and private surveyors who ensure accurate assessments. With Skylight, you can invest confidently, knowing every detail has been checked and validated. Our service eliminates uncertainties, giving you the clarity and peace of mind you need when investing in property.

Legal Opinion Services

Skylight ensures your property transactions are legally secure with our professional legal opinion services. Our team of experienced civil advocates and retired judges meticulously examines property documents, including title deeds and ownership records, to confirm their validity. We verify any potential legal disputes, encumbrances, or ownership claims, ensuring a risk-free purchase. By offering clear and detailed legal assessments, we help clients navigate the complexities of property laws. With Skylight, you gain the assurance of making legally sound property decisions, protecting your investments from unforeseen complications and disputes.

Land Survey Services

At Skylight, we provide precise land survey services to validate property measurements and boundaries. Our team of skilled surveyors, including retired Tahsildars and VAOs, uses advanced tools to ensure accuracy. Before purchase or development, we confirm that property dimensions align with official records and identify any encroachments. Our service is crucial for preventing future disputes and ensuring a smooth transaction. Whether you're buying, selling, or developing land, Skylight delivers reliable surveys to safeguard your investment. Trust us to provide the accuracy and insights you need for secure property dealings.

Revenue-Related Services

Skylight simplifies post-purchase property management with our revenue-related services. We assist clients in applying for Patta, Chitta, or Adangal to establish legal ownership. For busy or inexperienced property owners, we provide expert guidance to ensure a hassle-free process. While the application must be filed directly by the buyer, we handle the groundwork, from verifying documentation to coordinating with authorities. Skylight ensures your property rights are secured seamlessly, giving you peace of mind and clarity over your ownership status.

Property Maintenance Services

Our property maintenance services are designed for owners who are unable to personally oversee their properties. Skylight manages tenant engagement, maintenance tasks, and dispute resolution, ensuring your property remains secure and functional. For vacant land, we fence the property and verify encumbrance certificates to prevent encroachments. We also conduct monthly property inspections and provide detailed reports for transparency. Our services extend to gated communities and apartment complexes, ensuring a high standard of care. Skylight helps property owners maintain their assets without worry, delivering efficiency and reliability every step of the way.

Building Construction Services

At Skylight, we bring your construction dreams to life. Whether you're developing on vacant land or expanding an existing property, our team handles everything from planning and permits to execution. Our architects and engineers ensure compliance with local regulations while tailoring designs to your preferences. We conduct soil tests and structural assessments before starting construction to guarantee safety and durability. Skylight combines technical expertise with aesthetic excellence to deliver buildings that meet your vision. With us, you can trust every step of the process is handled with professionalism and precision.

Rental Management Services

Skylight offers comprehensive rental management services for property owners seeking steady income without the hassle of day-to-day oversight. We manage tenant screening, rent collection, and handling disputes, ensuring a seamless rental experience. Our team oversees maintenance and ensures the property remains in excellent condition. For NRIs and busy owners, we handle all responsibilities while providing regular updates. With Skylight, you can enjoy the financial benefits of renting without the stress, as we take care of everything from agreements to tenant relations.

Intellectual Property Services

Protect your creative and business assets with Skylight’s intellectual property services. We assist in the registration and litigation of trademarks, copyrights, designs, and patents, ensuring your intellectual property is legally safeguarded. Our team simplifies the complex processes of filing and defending IP claims, giving you the peace of mind to focus on innovation. Whether you're an entrepreneur, artist, or business owner, Skylight ensures your unique ideas and creations remain exclusively yours. With our support, your intellectual property is in trusted hands.

NRI Property Services

Services Skylight provides tailored property services for NRIs, ensuring seamless management and transactions in their absence. From rental management and tax payments to property monitoring and legal compliance, we handle it all. Our services include buying and selling properties, maintaining vacant lands, and securing them against encroachments. Skylight ensures transparency in every process, providing regular updates and reports. NRIs can trust us to manage their investments professionally and efficiently, offering peace of mind wherever they are in the world.

End-to-End Property Management

Our end-to-end property management services are designed for both NRIs and local property owners. Skylight handles all paperwork, tax payments, and property monitoring, ensuring compliance with legal requirements. We resolve disputes, verify property records, and conduct periodic inspections to keep your property in top condition. Our services extend to gated communities, where we manage tenant relations and property maintenance with the highest standards. Skylight offers a complete solution for hassle-free property ownership, allowing clients to focus on their priorities while we manage their investments.
